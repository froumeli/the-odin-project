Ntakos

Description

Ntakos, also known as Cretan dakos or Koukouvagia, is a traditional Greek salad hailing from the island of Crete.

Ingredients

  • 1 barley rusk
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • basil and thyme chopped
  • 1/2 fresh chopped onion
  • 100gr chopped feta chesse
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • olives
  • oregano

Steps

  1. Put the barley rusk on a plate.

  2. Chop the tomato and grate one half in a bowl. Dice the other half.

  3. In a bowl, put the grated tomato, vinegar, salt, peppet, basil and thyme and mix.

  4. Put the mixture on top of the rusk.

  5. Add the diced tomato, the chopped onion, the chopped feta chees and drizzle with olive oil.

  6. Serve with olives, pepper and oregano.
